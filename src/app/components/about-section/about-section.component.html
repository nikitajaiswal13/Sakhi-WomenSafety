About Sakhi

Sakhi is a platform dedicated to empowering women with access to safety resources, educational opportunities, and job prospects. Our mission is to create a secure environment where women can thrive and succeed in all areas of life.

We believe in providing the tools, knowledge, and support needed for women to build better futures. From safety alerts to scholarships and job listings, Sakhi is a companion every woman can rely on.

Sakhi strives to break barriers by making important resources accessible to women from all walks of life. Whether you're a student looking for scholarships, a professional seeking new job opportunities, or someone who needs a helping hand for personal safety, Sakhi is here to support you.
