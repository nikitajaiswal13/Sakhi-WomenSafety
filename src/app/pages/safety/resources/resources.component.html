How to File a Complaint

  1. Call the women's helpline (1091) or approach your nearest police station.
  2. You can file a First Information Report (FIR) under applicable sections of the law.
  3. Complaints can also be submitted online via National Commission for Women.
  4. Include necessary details, documents, and evidence wherever possible.
  5. Legal aid and counseling is often available through NGOs and state-run centers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Can I file a complaint anonymously?

Yes, some platforms allow anonymous complaints, especially for sensitive issues like harassment.

Q2: What if the police refuses to register an FIR?

You can escalate the issue to the Superintendent of Police or register online via official government portals.

Q3: Are there free legal services for women?

Yes, women can avail legal aid services through Legal Services Authorities at district and state levels.
